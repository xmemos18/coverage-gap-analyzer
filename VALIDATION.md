## ğŸ“‹ USER INTERVIEW SCRIPT

### Finding People
**Where to look:**
- Facebook Groups: "Snowbirds Florida", "RV Living Full Time"
- Reddit: r/snowbirds, r/digitalnomad, r/personalfinance
- Local: Retirement communities, RV parks
- Nextdoor: Post in snowbird-heavy areas

### Interview Questions (5-10 minutes)

**Introduction:**
```
Hi! I'm building a tool to help people who split time between multiple
states find the right health insurance. Can I ask you a few quick questions?
```

**Questions:**
1. **Do you split your time between multiple states? Which ones and for how long?**
   - Looking for: 2+ states, ~6 months each

2. **How do you currently handle health insurance?**
   - Looking for: Confusion, workarounds, "I don't know"

3. **What's the most frustrating part of dealing with insurance across states?**
   - Looking for: Provider networks, coverage gaps, confusion

4. **Have you ever had a situation where your insurance didn't cover you in one of your states?**
   - Looking for: Real pain points, money lost

5. **Have you ever skipped medical care because you weren't sure if you'd be covered?**
   - Looking for: Health risks taken due to confusion

6. **How did you choose your current insurance plan?**
   - Looking for: Guessing, agent recommendation, trial & error

7. **Would you use a free calculator that recommends the best insurance for your situation?**
   - Looking for: Immediate "yes!", enthusiasm

8. **What features would make you pay for a premium version?**
   - Options: Real-time plan prices, saved scenarios, enrollment help
   - Looking for: Willingness to pay, specific needs

9. **On a scale of 1-10, how painful is this problem for you?**
   - Looking for: 7+ scores

10. **Can I email you when the tool is ready?**
    - Collect email if interested
